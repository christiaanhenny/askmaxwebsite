Today

Removing friction from written interaction.

Everything that gets a polished message out the door — faster, in your voice.

  • Click. Prompt. Paste.

    Press ⌥ Space, talk for five seconds, polished draft pastes back where your cursor was.

  • Voice-first.

    Speech is ~3× faster than typing. Rambly “uhh tell sarah the spec is uhh delayed” becomes structured prose.

  • Works in every Mac app.

    Mail, Slack, WhatsApp, iMessage, LinkedIn, browser, Notes. One shortcut, every text field.

  • Reads what’s on your screen.

    The email thread, the recipient, the app — no manual copy-pasting of context.

  • Knows the app shape.

    No greeting in a Slack DM. Full structure in a cold email. One line in iMessage.

  • Sounds exactly like you.

    Greetings, sign-offs, sentence rhythm, the expressions you actually use — learned from your own messages.

  • Sharper every week.

    Every edit becomes a rule. Cross out “just circling back” twice — never written again.

Over time

Removing friction from building relationships.

Everything that keeps the relationship warm over time, not just in the moment.

  • Tracks to-do’s & to-get’s.Soon

    What you owe each person, what they owe you. Auto-extracted from sent and received messages.

  • Coached on attentiveness.

    “Tom’s marathon Saturday.” “Marloes just got promoted to VP.” “Q3 deal closes next week.” Surfaced when it matters.

  • Coached on deadlines.

    You promised Sarah Friday. PortaVox flags it Friday morning.

  • Builds rapport — by holding for you:
