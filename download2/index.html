The 30-second tax you stopped noticing.

Without PortaVox

  1. Switch to ChatGPT in a different tab.
  2. Copy the email or thread you’re replying to.
  3. Paste it into ChatGPT with a prompt.
  4. Wait for the response.
  5. Copy the response.
  6. Switch back to your inbox.
  7. Find where your cursor was.
  8. Paste, edit to sound like you, send.

With PortaVox

  1. Press ⌥ Space in the field you’re typing in.
  2. Speak or type what you want to say.
  3. Polished draft appears in your field. Send.

It already sees what’s on screen.

  • The thread. Reads the email, Slack message or DM you’re replying to — including selected text beyond the visible scroll area.
  • The recipient. Picks up the To: field, the chat header, the LinkedIn name — and pulls everything it remembers about that person.
  • The app. Slack DM gets no greeting. Cold email gets full structure. Tweet gets one line. Knows the difference automatically.
  • The exact field. Pastes back into the input where your cursor was. No clipboard dance. No app switching. No “wait where was I”.
