It already knows the situation.

Before drafting a single word, PortaVox checks five things — so the message lands like you spent an hour on it.

  • Your writing style.

    Greetings, sign-offs, sentence rhythm, the expressions you actually use — learned from your real messages.

  • Your document context.

    Every brief, deal memo, contract, and doc you’ve uploaded — pulled in when relevant, ignored when not.

  • The conversation history.

    What you talked about last time. What you decided. What you said you’d follow up on.

  • The recipient.

    Their name across apps, their nickname only you use, the facts you’ve shared about their life and work.

  • The app shape.

    No greeting in a Slack DM. Full structure in a cold email. One line in iMessage. Knows the difference.

  • Today, on this screen.

    Reads what you’re replying to. Sees the thread, the recipient, the context. No manual copy-pasting.
