How is this possible?

Portavox is the opposite of generic "AI tone." It learns from how you actually write — not tone presets, not copy-pasted samples. Just the words you've already sent.

  • Trained on your real writing. No sliders, no "make it more professional." It just watches what you send and matches it.
  • Knows the recipient. The way you talk to your sister isn't the way you talk to your CTO. Portavox keeps them straight — sign-offs, in-jokes, register, the works.
  • Sharper every week. Every reply you send teaches it more. After two weeks it stops sounding like AI; after a month, even close friends don't notice.
