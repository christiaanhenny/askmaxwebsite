End-to-end encrypted

The AI doesn't train on anything.

Your messages, your style, your relationship memory, encrypted on your device with a key only you hold. What reaches our servers is ciphertext; we don't have the key, and neither does anyone else. When PortaVox calls a model to draft, it runs under a Zero-Retention contract: deleted on return, never used to train.

How is this possible?

Two steps. Then it just keeps getting more you.

Step 1

Train the AI on your voice.

During onboarding you paste a handful of emails and messages that sound like you. From that moment on, PortaVox drafts in your voice: the same words, the same rhythm, the same level of warmth or directness.

Step 2

Hyper personal.

Per contact, per app, per situation, PortaVox keeps learning from your edits. You get drafts that fit the exact relationship and channel, in seconds.

Context, automatically

It sees what you see.

PortaVox quietly snapshots the window in front of you, reads who you're talking to and in which app, and uses that to ground the draft. No copy-pasting context. No switching tabs.
