Why it can write like you.

  • Per-relationship voice. “Best, Chris” to your investor. “Cheers C” to your co-founder. “C” to your sister.
  • Inside language. “Manny” only with your wife. “Boss” only with Tim. “Pao” only with Paolo.
  • Facts about them. Marloes is his wife. His daughter just started at Stanford. He coaches kids’ football on Tuesdays.
  • Edits become rules. Cross out “just circling back” twice. PortaVox never writes it again.
  • The thread on screen. Reads the email Sarah sent. Knows you’re in a Slack DM with engineering. Doesn’t greet a Slack thread.
  • Your context. Pulls the exact sentence from the brief you uploaded for this client — not a generic platitude.
